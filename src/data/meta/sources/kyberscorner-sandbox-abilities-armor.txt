Destiny 2: Monument of Triumph Sandbox, Abilities & Armor Preview

Monument of Triumph launches June 9, 2026, bringing one of Destiny 2’s largest sandbox updates, including new subclass abilities, ability reworks, Armor 3.0 additions, new armor archetypes, Exotic armor updates, and major balance changes.

This guide covers Bungie’s Dev Insights: Abilities & Armor Preview for Update 9.7.0.

## New Subclass Abilities

### Titan Sunbreaker: Shieldburst

Shieldburst is a new Solar Titan Aspect that enhances Barricade with Solar functionality.

Towering Barricade ↓▸ Barricade slides forward across the ground before stopping▸ Lets Titans advance while protected

Rally Barricade ↓▸ Standing behind the barricade grants scorching rounds▸ Applies to Solar and Kinetic weapons for you and allies

Detonation Effect ↓▸ While the barricade is deployed, activate class ability again to detonate it▸ Detonation scorches enemies caught in the blast▸ Dealing damage increases the power of the explosion

Build Notes ↓▸ Works with Arbor Warden, Hallowfire Heart, and Khepri’s Horn▸ Damage is buffed by Roaring Flames▸ Can create Sunspots with Sol Invictus▸ Damage scales with Class stat above 100

## Hunter Gunslinger: Crackshot

Crackshot is a new Solar Hunter Aspect built around a quickdraw gunslinger fantasy.

How It Works ↓▸ Aim at targets to mark them▸ Activate class ability to fire up to three scorching shots▸ If one target is marked, all three shots hit that target▸ If multiple targets are marked, shots split between them▸ Landing all three shots cures you

Build Notes ↓▸ Can be used while airborne▸ Damage scales with Class stat above 100▸ Strong pairings include Radiant Dance Machines and The Sixth Coyote▸ Scorch can combine with other scorch sources to trigger Ignitions▸ Counts toward On Your Mark and Gunpowder Gamble progress

## Warlock Voidwalker: Soul Siphon

Soul Siphon is a new Void Warlock Aspect focused on draining enemies and gaining survivability.

How It Works ↓▸ Ready the Aspect by hitting an enemy with a powered melee▸ Once ready, press melee again to siphon energy from enemies in front of you▸ Deals damage while gathering Void Overshield and class ability energy

Build Notes ↓▸ Pocket Singularity has two charges by default, improving uptime▸ Building into Melee increases uptime and damage▸ Pairs with Felwinter’s Helm, Claws of Ahamkara, Karnstein Armlets, and Nezarec’s Sin▸ Works well with Feed the Void for Devour uptime▸ Class energy gain pairs well with Child of the Old Gods

## New Darkness Grenades

### Stasis Shatter Grenade

A Stasis grenade that sticks to enemies before exploding into ricocheting Stasis shrapnel.

Effects ↓▸ Sticks to combatants▸ Explodes into Stasis shrapnel▸ Shrapnel ricochets off surfaces▸ Direct hits freeze combatants▸ Slows enemy players▸ Frozen targets shatter instantly and take bonus damage

Important Champion Update ↓▸ Stasis Shatter will once again stun Unstoppable Champions▸ Shatter Grenade can stun Unstoppable Champions immediately on detonation

Build Notes ↓▸ Strong in enclosed spaces▸ Effective against grouped enemies▸ Has unique interaction with Hunter Revenant’s Touch of Winter Aspect

### Strand Slicewire Grenade

A Strand grenade that bounces and releases whirling threads.

Effects ↓▸ Bounces along the ground▸ Releases multiple whirling Strand lashes▸ Damages enemies up to four times▸ Severs targets, reducing their damage output

Build Notes ↓▸ Strong damage option when multiple lashes connect▸ Effective against grouped enemies▸ Has unique functionality with Broodweaver’s Mindspun Invocation Aspect

## New Hunter Nightstalker Melee

### Phantom Surge

Phantom Surge gives Nightstalker Hunters a new aggressive melee option beyond Smoke Bomb.

How It Works ↓▸ Dash forward through enemies▸ Slash combatants as you pass through them▸ Deals increased damage to Void-debuffed targets▸ Deals increased damage to targets marked by On the Prowl▸ Generates Void Overshield for each combatant hit▸ Defeating at least one combatant grants more Overshield and refunds melee energy

Important Smoke Bomb Update ↓▸ Smoke Bomb now makes you and nearby allies invisible by default▸ Vanishing Step is no longer required for Smoke Bomb invisibility

## Major Ability Reworks

### Hunter Nightstalker: Trapper’s Ambush

Trapper’s Ambush has been reworked into a more aggressive Void damage tool.

New Functionality ↓▸ Uses class ability energy instead of melee energy▸ Activate air move to dive to the ground▸ Damages targets on impact▸ Damage is greatly increased while you have a positive Void status effect▸ Damaging enemies heals you▸ Defeating combatants grants Devour▸ Casting while invisible refreshes invisibility on impact

Build Notes ↓▸ Activates class ability perks such as Marksman’s Dodge and Gambler’s Dodge▸ Can combo with Phantom Surge▸ Retains smoke-cloud behavior when paired with Vanishing Step

### Titan Sentinel: Ward of Dawn

Ward of Dawn has been fully reworked into a larger offensive and defensive combat space.

New Functionality ↓▸ Bubble is more than twice its previous size▸ Allies inside gain Void Overshield▸ Allies inside gain increased weapon damage▸ Enemies inside are weakened▸ Ward owner gains increased melee damage inside▸ Nearby enemies are pulled into the dome on deployment▸ Base combatants are taunted by players inside▸ Ward core can be picked up and thrown to reposition the Ward up to two times

PvP Note ↓▸ Ward’s core acts as its weak point in Crucible

Helm of Saint-14 Synergy ↓▸ Reworked to support the new Ward of Dawn▸ Extends Ward duration▸ Lets allies take Weapons of Light outside the dome

### Warlock Broodweaver: Mindspun Invocation

Mindspun Invocation now changes charged Threadling Grenade into a Threadling Nest.

New Functionality ↓▸ Hold grenade input to charge Threadling Grenade▸ Creates a Threadling Nest at the impact location▸ Nest repeatedly spawns Threadlings▸ Acts like a Threadling turret on the battlefield

Build Notes ↓▸ Interacts with Service of Luzaku Exotic Machine Gun

## Stasis Harvest & Frost Armor Updates

### Shatter Shards

All Stasis subclasses can now create Shatter Shards intrinsically.

Shatter Shards are created when ↓▸ A Stasis Guardian shatters a Stasis Crystal▸ A Stasis Guardian shatters a frozen enemy▸ A Stasis Guardian defeats a slowed or frozen enemy

### Harvest Aspects

Tectonic Harvest, Grim Harvest, and Glacial Harvest have been reworked.

Updated Harvest Benefits ↓▸ Picking up a Shatter Shard grants Frost Armor▸ While you have Frost Armor, Stasis or Kinetic weapon kills can shatter enemies▸ While you have Frost Armor, taking damage grants ability energy

Class Energy Gains ↓▸ Titan: Melee energy▸ Warlock: Grenade energy▸ Hunter: Class ability energy

Important Change ↓▸ Harvest Aspects reduced from three Fragment slots to two

### Frost Armor

Frost Armor now has better uptime and smoother decay.

Changes ↓▸ Frost Armor stacks melt off one at a time instead of all at once▸ Each stack gives slightly more damage resistance▸ Damage resistance at five stacks increased from about 31% to 35%▸ Whisper of Rime extends stack duration from five seconds to six seconds

## Ability Balance Highlights

### Global Ability Stat Changes

Bungie is reducing the ceiling for highly optimized ability uptime.

Changes ↓▸ Maximum passive ability cooldown bonus from stats reduced by about 20%▸ Maximum active ability cooldown bonus from stats reduced from 190% to 125%▸ Ability uptime floor remains similar for less optimized builds▸ Highly optimized builds will see the largest reduction

### Super Energy Changes

Boss Damage Super Energy ↓▸ Super energy gained from dealing damage to bosses reduced by 60%

Bungie says this is intended to reduce Super spam during boss damage phases and shift more focus back toward weapons and full damage rotations.

## Buildcrafting Highlights

Key Takeaways ↓

▸ Class stat is more important than ever, with several damage-dealing class abilities now scaling when your Class stat exceeds 100

▸ Stasis builds receive major improvements through intrinsic Shatter Shards, Harvest Aspect reworks, and Frost Armor buffs

▸ Armor 3.0 gains six new archetypes, making it easier to target specific stat combinations

▸ Ghost Armorer mods now have improved odds of rolling your preferred archetype

▸ All Armor 3.0 Exotic armor now has Tier 5 stats and access to all tuning mods

▸ New armor set bonuses provide more buildcrafting options across destinations, raids, dungeons, and core activities

▸ Ability uptime has been reduced for highly optimized builds, while weapon damage becomes a larger part of endgame combat

▸ Super generation from boss damage has been reduced, making damage phases less dependent on chaining multiple Supers

▸ New subclass abilities and reworks create fresh build opportunities across Solar, Void, Stasis, and Strand subclasses
